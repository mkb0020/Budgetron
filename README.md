# Budgetron — A Local-First Budgeting App for People With a Day Job and a Side Hustle

**Budgetron is a free, offline desktop budgeting app built for people juggling a full-time paycheck and a growing side business** — indie developers, freelancers, Etsy sellers, itch.io creators, streamers, and anyone else with income coming from more than one place. No subscriptions, no cloud account, no ads. Your financial data stays on your machine.

---

## What Budgetron Does

Most budgeting apps assume one income source and a generic list of categories. Budgetron assumes something more specific and more common than people admit: **a W-2 paycheck funding a business that isn't profitable yet.** It answers one question every time you open it:

> **"Given my current finances, how much can I confidently spend, save, and invest in my business this month?"**

It does this by combining:

- **Real take-home pay math** — federal tax brackets, FICA, pre-tax retirement and HSA contributions, and post-tax benefits, so you're budgeting off your actual net pay instead of a rough guess.
- **Living expense tracking** — recurring and variable household costs in one editable list.
- **A grocery spending tracker** — log the total from each trip (amounts vary, the app does the averaging) and get a real monthly average instead of a category you never trust.
- **Business overhead and one-time purchases** — separate from personal expenses, so you can see exactly what your business is costing you.
- **Side-hustle revenue logging** — track payouts from itch.io, Etsy, Gumroad, Patreon, or anywhere else money comes in.
- **Business savings goals with progress tracking** — new equipment, a booth fee, a subscription you're saving up to afford.
- **An allocation dashboard** — the one screen that pulls it all together into what's actually free to spend, save, or reinvest.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Budgetron free?**
Yes. No subscription, no in-app purchases, no account required.

**Does Budgetron require an internet connection?**
No. Budgetron is fully local-first and works entirely offline. Your financial data never leaves your computer.

**What platforms does Budgetron support?**
Budgetron is a native desktop app built with Tauri, supporting Windows, macOS, and Linux.

**Is my financial data private?**
Yes. There's no cloud sync, no analytics, and no external server. All data is stored locally on your device.

**Can Budgetron calculate my actual take-home pay?**
Yes. It estimates federal income tax and FICA based on your gross salary, filing status, pre-tax retirement/HSA contributions, and post-tax benefits, then breaks down your net pay per paycheck and per month.

**Does Budgetron support tracking a side business separately from personal finances?**
Yes. Budgetron has a dedicated Business tab for recurring overhead, one-time purchases, and business savings goals — kept separate from personal living expenses.

**How does the grocery tracker work?**
Instead of estimating a fixed monthly grocery budget, you log the total from each trip as it happens. Budgetron calculates both a per-trip average and a true monthly average based on the actual calendar time between your logged trips.

**Is Budgetron a replacement for accounting or tax software?**
No. Budgetron is a personal budgeting and cash-flow planning tool, not tax preparation or bookkeeping software. Its tax estimates are for planning purposes only — consult a tax professional or your actual paystub for exact figures.
